Get to know us

Our Products

Explore our extensive range of products

GRAIN STORAGE INSECTICIDE

Pesto Grain Storage Insecticide has been successfully used for many years across the United States and Australia. It has always been used as an additive to the grain body as well as a pre-bin spray prior to loading the storage and/or vessel.



CRAWLING INSECT KILLER

Pesto Crawling Insect Killer is an all natural "organic" , broad-spectrum , desiccant that has been proven effective against a wide range of crawling insects. It's both safer for animal and human health than competing inorganic pesticides.



FEED ADDITIVE

Pesto Feed Additive mixed in the feed, coats the feed, allowing it to "flow" better. Our product protects feed from mold, and aids in the animal's digestion. This translates to less wasted feed and healthier animals.
